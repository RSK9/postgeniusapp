Do I need any technical skills to use PostGenius? +
None at all. If you can type a sentence, you can use PostGenius. It's built for busy business owners, not developers or marketers.
Can I cancel my subscription anytime? +
Yes. Cancel with one click from your settings page, no questions asked. You'll keep full access until the end of your billing period.
What types of businesses is this built for? +
Any local or online business — restaurants, cafes, gyms, beauty salons, real estate agents, online stores, coaches, consultants, freelancers. If you post on social media to find customers, PostGenius is for you.
How is PostGenius different from just using ChatGPT? +
ChatGPT is a general tool that requires careful, lengthy prompting to get good social content — and you have to re-explain your business every single time. PostGenius is purpose-built for social media, knows the best practices for each platform, remembers your brand voice and business details, and is five times faster to use day-to-day.
Will the posts actually sound like me? +
Yes. That's one of the things we're most proud of. When you set up your brand voice in settings, PostGenius uses that to keep every post consistent with how you actually talk to your customers. Most users say the posts sound more like them than what they'd write themselves, because they finally have time to think about the message rather than battling with grammar.
Is there a free trial? +
Yes — two ways. First, the free Starter plan gives you 10 posts per month with no credit card required, forever. Second, the Pro plan includes a 7-day free trial before you're charged anything.
